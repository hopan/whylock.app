# Whylock

> A mindful pause before every phone unlock.

Whylock intercepts every phone unlock with a short breathing animation, then asks you to write down why you're picking up your phone. Over time, you see the difference between intentional use and mindless habit.

---

## How It Works

1. You unlock your phone
2. A breathing ring appears — follow it for a few seconds
3. Write your reason in one sentence
4. Proceed, intentionally

Every unlock is logged. Your stats show intentional rate, skips, and top reasons over time.

---

## Features

- **Breathing overlay** — a short pause on every unlock
- **Reason input** — articulate your intent before diving in
- **Stats screen** — 7-day / 30-day trends, day-by-day breakdown
- **Reminders** — a personal note shown at each unlock
- **Skip option** — 3-tap bypass for genuine urgency
